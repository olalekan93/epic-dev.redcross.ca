In Bangalpara of Ashtagram, the people of Howar had to suffer because of the lack of a bridge across the Meghna river connecting the two districts. During the monsoon there was no option of communication except by boat. The government has taken the initiative of constructing a paved bridge over Meghna River in Noagaon of Bangalpara Union and Chatalpara New Market area of \u200b\u200bNasirnagar Upazila of Brahmanbaria District to remove the inconvenience of Haor residents. If implemented, the project will usher in new horizons in road connectivity in Howrah. Speed \u200b\u200bwill come in the economy, along with it, the standard of living of Haor people will increase.<\/p>\n

According to local residents, Haor is a remote area, so if you want to travel to the district town or Dhaka city, you have to spend one night in a launch or trawler and come back to the village the next day. If the bridge is constructed, the various districts of the country including Brahmanbaria district can be connected by road throughout the year. As a result, I will play a special role in getting the fair price of Haor’s agricultural products and fresh water fish.<\/p>\n
