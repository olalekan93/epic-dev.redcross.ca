On Friday (March 8) around 10 o’clock in the night, these leaders and activists joined the election public meeting focusing on the upcoming Sadar Upazila elections in Kulpaddi area of \u200b\u200bMadaripur Municipality. Before this, several activists joined Shajahan Khan’s group.<\/p>\n

Shajahan Khan, a member of the presidium of the party, spoke as the chief guest at the election public meeting organized by the 5th ward of the Awami League municipality. <\/p>\n

Babu Uttam Kumar Poddar, Md. Kabir Hossain, Abul Kasem Sharif, Md. Montu Khan, Uchhbas Hossain and half a hundred activists joined Awami League’s Shajahan Khan’s group with bouquets of flowers. The meeting was chaired by the president of the 5th ward of the municipality, Abdul Kalam Akon. <\/p>\n

The meeting was conducted by a member of the Municipal Jubo League. Rubel Khan and General Secretary of Sadar Upazila Chhatra League Ripon Howladar conducted.<\/p>\n

Shajahan Khan gave a speech highlighting various development promises of the government. Later, he introduced his eldest son Asibur Rahman Khan to everyone in the upcoming Sadar Upazila elections.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
